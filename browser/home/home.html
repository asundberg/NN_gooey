Welcome to the BrainLab!

What's a BrainLab?

 The BrainLab is your own personal neural network builder. Whether you are a newbie or a machine learning Phd, let the BrainLab be a convenient way for you to train and test your own neural networks.

A neural network? That sounds intimidating.

 It is! But guiding new people through the process of training their own network is one of our goals here. You will find helpful hints and descriptions throughout the site.

You still haven't explained what a neural network is.

 It's an algorithm for creating a model that can be used to predict something. For example, a doctor is deciding between two different treatments for a patient for a particular ailment. She takes data from previous patients treated for this ailment and trains a model. Then she feeds the current patient's data into the model, and it can give a best guess for the right treatment.
