What's a BrainLab?

 The BrainLab is your own personal neural network builder. Whether you are a newbie or a machine learning Phd, let the BrainLab be a convenient way for you to train and test your own neural networks.

A neural network? That sounds intimidating.

 It is! But guiding new people through the process of training their own network is one of our goals here. You will find helpful hints and descriptions throughout the site.
